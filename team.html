Who We Are

We are seasoned professionals with decades of experience at the intersection of business and technology. We have a successful track record in helping companies achieve real, measurable growth.

Steve Tover

CEO & Founder

Steven Tover, the founder and CEO of Stage 2 Partners, has worked in the hi-tech startup sector as an entrepreneur, employee, executive manager, consultant and strategic advisor. He develops go-to market strategies as well as distribution channels for a range of clients, and assists in raising capital.

Prior to Stage 2, Steve founded and was the CEO of Net Talk, one of the early multipoint VoIP providers, and he was a founder and CEO of Softcare, a disease management and patient engagement software provider. Steve established Stage 2 Partners to leverage his experience to facilitate the development of early stage companies and take them to the next level in their businesses.

William Weisel

Business Development

William Weisel serves as a business development consultant for Stage 2 Partners, and is involved in other various projects for technology companies. He is a lecturer at the Haifa University Graduate School of Management and teaches a course entitled Business and Law Convergence that focuses on the practical issues of managing the business affairs of modern companies from a legal perspective.

Licensed to practice law in California and Israel, Mr. Weisel served as general counsel, and was a member of senior management, of four publicly-traded, multinational, hi-tech Israeli companies over the past two decades, and now brings that experience to both established and emerging businesses.

Zahava Bogner

Creative

Zahava Bogner serves as the creative consultant for branding and visual solutions for Stage 2 Partners. Zahava and her team at Zatar Creative collaborate with us to implement innovative visual marketing solutions for our clients. With over 25 years of corporate design experience, Zahava has experience in a wide range of business sectors, including: business-to-business, culinary arts, education, finance, healthcare, hi-tech, not-for-profit, and retail. Together with Stage 2 Partners, Zahava assesses data to determine relevant concepts and strategies to simultaneously build and exploit brand equity and meet current business and marketing objectives.

Dan F

Technical

Dan has worked on the cutting edge of Big Data and Intelligence for 15 years. He served with distinction in IDF Elite Intelligence Unit 8200 as a linguist and analyst of mission-critical, real-time intelligence during a period of intense conflict. After earning a Bachelor's degree in East Asian languages, he worked as a data analyst for one of the largest online gaming companies in the world during a critical expansion of the company into several new lines of business. Dan then joined a pioneering Intelligence company in designing and bringing to market one of the first end-to-end solutions for collecting, analyzing and operationalizing internet open source data and deep web data at the tier-1 government level. Dan consults with Stage2Partners on matters related to product, product positioning and roadmap, and other technical aspects of the business.
