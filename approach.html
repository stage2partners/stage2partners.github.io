OUR GUIDING PHILOSOPHY

Stage 2 Partners was established to facilitate the development of early stage companies and get them to the next level in their business. In some cases this means developing a go-to market strategy; in others it means developing distribution channels; and in others it means assisting my clients to raise capital. At Stage 2 Partners, we drive results through customized services and cross-functional expertise in strategic planning, marketing, business development, revenue generation, and operational controls. Internal capabilities and leadership skills are honed at every level. We accelerate our clients' success by identifying key issues to provide cost-effective, value-added services and solutions.

  • Strategy

    Developing the right strategy for your business means making decisions that will allow your company to achieve its long-term objectives. And for every business there can be more than one "right" strategy to meet those objectives. We help you understand how you fit in your market or industry, identify where you want to be, and then jointly create the right plan on how to get you there.

  • Business Development

    At the end of the day, itâ€™s all about creating the right opportunities and partnerships that will grow revenues. We actively help you target key business opportunities, and engage with strategic partners that will drive sales.

  • Marketing

    Your company's unique brand is the way your customers and partners identify who you are and what you do, and differentiate you from competitors. We help you create a distinctive and definitive brand as a guide in the development and implementation of your go-to-market programs.

  • Operations

    Creating the right operations environment is an ongoing and challenging task for any size organization. We work with your management team to establish effective processes and procedures to fine tune your corporate and operational structure for its optimum value, efficiency and potential growth.
